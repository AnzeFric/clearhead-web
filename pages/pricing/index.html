Questions

Frequently asked

Is there a free trial?
Yes — you can explore the app for free before subscribing. No credit card required to get started.
Can I switch between plans?
Absolutely. You can upgrade to annual or switch to monthly at any time through your App Store subscription settings.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep full access until the end of your billing period. After that, your data is preserved but premium features are paused.
How is payment handled?
All billing is managed securely through Apple's App Store via RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment details.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your health data stays on your device and is never sold or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Do you offer refunds?
Refund requests are handled by Apple directly via the App Store. Contact us at techmg103@gmail.com and we'll do our best to help.
